About Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor. He was discovered through his YouTube videos in 2008 and quickly rose to fame with his debut single "One Time". Bieber has since become one of the world's best-selling music artists, known for hit songs like "Baby", "Sorry", and "Love Yourself".
